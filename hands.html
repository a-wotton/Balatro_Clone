Hand Types - Explained

  • This game contains all of the hands that you would find in the game of Poker.
  • Their hierarchy is the same is the same as they would be in Poker, except higher hands provide a higher score multiplier. See the hierarchy below.

Here are all the hand types explained in ascending order:

  • High Card

    Mult: 1x

    There is only one card played.

  • Pair

    Mult: 2x

    Two cards of the same value.

  • Two Pair

    Mult: 2x

    Two different pairs.

  • Three-of-a-Kind

    Mult: 3x

    Three cards of the same value.

  • Straight

    Mult: 4x

    Five cards with consecutive values.

  • Flush

    Mult: 4x

    Five cards of the same suit.

  • Full House

    Mult: 4x

    A pair and a three-of-a-kind.

  • Four-of-a-Kind

    Mult: 7x

    Four cards of the same value.

  • Straight Flush

    Mult: 8x

    A straight where all cards are of the same suit.

  • Royal Flush

    Mult: 10x

    The highest possible straight flush. Ace, King, Queen, Jack and Ten, all of the same suit.
